Michigan Neuroprosthetics

We are Michigan Neuroprosthetics, a student-run design team at the University of Michigan building low-cost, lightweight, and customizable prosthetic arms for children.

ABOUT US

We are committed to making designs that are

LOW COST

LIGHTWEIGHT

CUSTOMIZABLE

OPEN-SOURCE

We design the arms to be less than $100, and components can be reused as the wearer grows.

The arms are 0.5-1.5 lbs, designed for both durability and mobility.

The arm is custom fit to each user and can be designed specific to the wearer's needs.

We're built on collaborative development to ensure the best wuality possible.
